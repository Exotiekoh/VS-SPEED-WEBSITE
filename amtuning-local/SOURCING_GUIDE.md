## Part 1: Sourcing Products

### Recommended Platforms

| Platform | Best For | Shipping Time | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AliExpress** | Consumer goods, small parts | 15-45 days | Good for ignition coils, sensors, small accessories |
| **Alibaba** | Bulk orders, OEM parts | 20-60 days | Better prices at quantity, requires negotiation |
| **Amazon** | Fast shipping items | 2-7 days | Higher cost but fast, good for priority orders |
| **eBay** | Rare/discontinued parts | Varies | Good for specific fitments |

### Sourcing Workflow

1. **Customer places order** on VSSPEED Global
2. **Order appears** in `/admin/orders` dashboard
3. **Copy supplier email template** from dashboard
4. **Place order** with supplier (AliExpress, etc.)
5. **Update order status** to "Forwarded"
6. **Track shipment** and update customer

## Part 4: Supplier Vetting Checklist

Before adding a supplier:

- [ ] Verified reviews (4+ stars)
- [ ] Ships to North America
- [ ] Responds within 24 hours
- [ ] Provides tracking numbers
- [ ] Has return policy
- [ ] Quality photos (real product)
- [ ] Test order placed and received

## Part 7: Quality Assurance

### Before Listing a Product

1. Order sample for yourself
2. Test fitment on target vehicle
3. Take real photos
4. Write accurate description
5. Set realistic expectations

### After Customer Receives

1. Follow up for feedback
2. Request photos for social proof
3. Address any issues immediately
4. Offer replacement if defective
